Yangyuxuan Kang works as an AI Research Scientist at Intel. 
His research interests lie at the intersection of 3D Computer Vision and Large Language Model. Specifically, he focuses on **digital human**, including 3D human reconsturction, pose estimation, and action recognition. In the era of large foundation models, he is also deeply engaged in **efficienct LLM inference**, doing research on model compression and high-performance serving systems. Additionally, he also has extensive experience with **knowledge graph and recommendation systems**.

Previously, he obtained PhD in Computer Science from [Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ISCAS)](https://is.cas.cn/), supervised by Prof. [Enhua Wu](https://people.ucas.edu.cn/~ehwu).
