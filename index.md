Yangyuxuan Kang works as an AI Research Scientist at Intel. 
His research interests span many aspects of **digital human** including **3D human reconsturction & pose estimation & action recognition**. Among the large model era, he is also interested in **efficienct LLM inference** including **model compression** and **serving system**. He also has deep understanding of **knowledge graph and retrieval systems**.

Previously, he completed his PhD in Computer Science at [Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences](https://is.cas.cn/), supervised by [Enhua Wu](https://people.ucas.edu.cn/~ehwu). Before that, he obtained his bachelor degree at Chengdu University of Technology and **Outstanding Graduate Award of Sichuan**.
